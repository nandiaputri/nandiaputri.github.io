Domestic Waste Volume in Bandung City



From June to July 2021, I had the opportunity to do an internship at Esri Indonesia in a program called Esri Indonesia Fellowship Program. During this program, I did a project to mapping the estimation of domestic waste volume in Bojonegara Development Region, Bandung City (2021, 2026, & 2031). The results of mapping can help policy makers (Dinas Kebersihan Kota Bandung) to plan the priority of waste management services and to evaluate the sustainability of waste infrastructure. This project is made in the form of an Esri Indonesia storymap which can be accessed HERE.

In addition, during this internship I also created a dashboard that displays information on the volume of domestic waste in the form of a map equipped with graphs and charts.

This project aims to determine:
  1. the estimation of Bojonegara domestic waste volume in 2021
  2. the projected volume of domestic waste in the next five to ten years, and
  3. the capacity of each TPS for evaluating the needed of garbage infrastructure in the future
This project is carried out by utilizing a geographic information system for domestic waste through Esri products (ArcGIS Online, ArcGIS Pro, and Survey123)

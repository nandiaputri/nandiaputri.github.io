Flooding Risk in Bojonegoro Regency




In 2021, along with M. Zidan, Vira Islami, and Raisa Putri, we produce a paper "Analisis Bahaya dan Kerentanan Wilayah Menggunakan Metode Overlay Sebagai Upaya Penanggulangan Bencana Banjir di Kabupaten Bojonegoro". This paper achieve a Grantee of Program Kreativitas Mahasiswa-Artikel Ilmiah (PKM-AI). This research is aim to analyze flooding risk and vulnerability in Bojonegoro Regency with spatial analysis overlay and scoring method. Flooding risk parameter maps used in this research consist of:
  1. Slope Map
  2. Elevation Map
  3. Distance to Rivers Map
  4. Surface Runoff Map
  5. Rainfall Map

Correlation Analysis of Land Surface Temperature and Land Cover in Surabaya City




In 2020, along with Vira Islami, and Naufal Hafizhan, we produce a paper about "Analisis Pengaruh Perubahan Tutupan Lahan Terhadap Distribusi Suhu Permukaan Menggunakan Citra Landsat Multi-Waktu di Kota Surabaya". Rapid population growth in the City of Surabaya has led the uncontrolled conversion of land cover. Changes in land cover will affect the distribution of surface temperatures. Changes in temperature affect the micro and macro climates that change other climatic conditions. This study aims to analyze the effect of land cover change on the distribution of surface temperature using landsat 5 (1999) and landsat 8 (2019) images in the city of Surabaya. Land cover types are obtained by the supervised classification of the maximum likelihood method. The temperature value data is obtained by combining the emissivity value of the land cover type with the spectral value on the Landsat thermal image channel using the Single-Channel logarithm.

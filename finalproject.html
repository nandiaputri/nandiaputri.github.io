Bachelor's Degree Final Project





In 2022, as a final student in my college i need to take a bachelor's final project. The research for my final project is analyze the effectiveness of on-screen digitizing and Object-based Image Analysis (OBIA) for mapping informal and formal settlement land using drone aerial imagery in Ciwaruga Village.

This village has the potential to result in changes in the use and ownership of the land in settlement areas quickly. This condition has an impact on the efforts to monitor the ownership of the land which needs to be fixed by cadastral data updates. The process of mapping the location, boundaries, and area of the land in settlement areas is inseparable from several obstacles. Specially mapping with the direct technique method such as terrestrial or satellite surveys has limitations and constraints in terms of technical / methods, time, cost, and human resources. These obstacles can be overcome by indirect technical mapping methods using aerial imagery from drones. This approach allows for faster mapping and updating of cadastral data for wider area coverage. There are two methods for cadastral mapping using aerial imagery, i.e., on-screen digitizing and object-based image analysis (OBIA).

My research will analyze the effectiveness of two methods in mapping informal and formal settlement land using drone aerial imagery. Based on a comparison of accuracy & distance planimetric errors, the number of lands, and the form of the field produced, it can be said that mapping with on-screen digitazing is foremost to the OBIA method. However, OBIA was equally good as a semi-automatic cadastral mapping algorithm, with land extraction accuracy assessments showing high values of 0.99 in informal settlements and 0.91 in informal settlements. From this study it can be concluded that the screen digitized cadastral map can be regarded as a parcel map in the land certificate, as it meets the tolerances based on the plane error and distance specified by the BPN.

Neraca Penatagunaan Tanah in Majalaya




From September to November 2021, I had the opportunity to do an internship at Kementerian ATR/BPN Kantor Pertanahan Kabupaten Bandung. As a GIS Analyst Intern at Substansi Penatagunaan Tanah, I am tasked with being an assistant to a BPN employee in making maps of Neraca Penatagunaan Tanah as a database for spatial planning analysis.

Neraca penatagunaan tanah is a GIS database containing changes in land use over a certain period of time, the suitability of current land use to the RTRW (Rencana Tata Ruang Wilayah), and land availability based on land use, land tenure, and spatial planning. Neraca Penatagunaan Tanah created in ArcGIS Desktop and consist of:
  1. Land Use Map in two different time periods (existing land use and a few years ago)
  2. Land Tenure Map
  3. Regional Spatial Plan Map
  4. Land Use Suitability Map according to RTRW
  5. Land Availability Map

Land Technical Considerations Map



From September to November 2021, I had the opportunity to do an internship at Kementerian ATR/BPN Kantor Pertanahan Kabupaten Bandung. As a GIS Analyst Intern at Substansi Penatagunaan Tanah, I am tasked with being an assistant to a BPN employee in making maps of land technical considerations for kesesuaian kegiatan pemanfaatan ruang (KKPR).

Land technical consideration maps consist of:
  1. Location Map
  2. Land Use Map
  3. Land Capability Map
  4. Land Tenure Map
  5. Regional Spatial Plan Map
  6. Land Use Suitability Map
  7. Land Availability Map
  8. Land Technical Consideration Map
